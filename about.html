About the site

Learning AI clearly.

Novera is an independent blog by Daniel about using AI tools in practical, everyday ways. The focus is on ChatGPT, Codex, prompts, experiments, and simple guides that help readers understand what these tools can actually do.

What Novera Covers

This site is being built around first-hand learning. That means posts will come from trying tools, building small projects, testing prompts, comparing workflows, and writing down what was useful, confusing, surprising, or worth avoiding.

The goal is to make AI feel easier to understand for beginners, creators, students, small business owners, and anyone who wants to use tools like ChatGPT and Codex more confidently.
